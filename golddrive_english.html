Our Approach to Instructor Selection
is Our Advantage!

Direct selection: Each instructor undergoes theoretical training, and after completing the exam, they go through an extensive practical probation period to refine their skills. Only after this phase do they begin working at GOLD DRIVE driving school.

Professionalism and patience: These are some of the key factors that make our instructors outstanding. Thanks to their polite and friendly attitude, the lessons are conducted in a calm and comfortable atmosphere.

Teaching Skills: All our instructors are not only proficient drivers but are also experienced educators and psychologists. They know how to effectively transfer their knowledge to students so that results are achieved within a short period.

Individual approach: Each student is treated individually, with an emphasis on their abilities. Thanks to this tailored teaching method, the lessons are as effective as possible.

Continuous learning: Our instructors never stop learning. They regularly attend refresher courses and are constantly exploring new techniques and teaching methods.
